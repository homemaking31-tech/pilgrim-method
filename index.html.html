The Pilgrim Method

Seven steps that move Scripture from the page into your heart

P
Pause & Prepare
Begin with prayer. Still your heart and invite God into your study before you read a word.
I
Immerse in the Text
Read the passage slowly and fully. Let the words wash over you before analysing anything.
L
Look Closely
Observe what is actually there. Who, what, when, where — see what the text truly says.
G
Gather Context
Step outside the passage. Understand its history, culture, and place in the larger story.
R
Relate Across
Find other verses that speak to the same truth. Let Scripture interpret Scripture.
I
Interpret Carefully
Ask what the author intended. Seek the meaning God breathed into these words.
M
Make it Personal
Apply it. Let Scripture do its transforming work in your everyday, ordinary life.
